ZK-rollups scale Ethereum by processing thousands of transactions off-chain, then submitting a single cryptographic proof (a SNARK or STARK) to the mainnet. Because mathematics validates the proof instantly, it inherits Layer 1 security without needing to verify every transaction individually.
